Three ways to Benefit Educators

Omni Nano Discover

The Discover program is a free workshop offered to high schools, perfect for exposing audiences of young adults to the promise and potential of nanotechnology. The on-site workshop provides students a hands-on introduction to basic concepts in nanotechnology, includes stimulating demonstrations and videos, offering students unique insights into recent innovations and applications for nanotechnology!

• Speakers and materials for this program are provided by Omni Nano.

Ideal for audiences of up to 40 people.

Omni Nano Bridges

The Bridges program provides more in-depth exposure to the science of nanotechnology, and includes more detailed and challenging experiments. The Bridges program is suitable for larger groups of students, and provides an extended program for usually up to a full day.

• Materials and oversight for this program are provided by Omni Nano, but a hands-on partnership with teachers at participating schools is required.

Ideal for audiences of up to 200 people.

Omni Nano Curriculum

The flagship Omni Nano program is the comprehensive curriculum tailored for the needs of science educators. The Omni Nano curriculum provides an award-winning set of course materials, including syllabus, lecture outlines, lecture notes, slideshows, videos and quizzes. Our curriculum supports educators and provides a flexible framework for customizing a course introducing high school students to nanotechnology, including the science and applications.

The Omni Nano curriculum is available across digital devices, which helps educators accommodate the different ways students access information today. We support the educators, by empowering them with the ability to customize an engaging, high-impact course on nanotechnology.

Developed for classroom instruction.
