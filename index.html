Three ways to Benefit Educators

Omni Nano Discover

The Discover program is a free 45-minute workshop where Omni Nano sends one of our trained nanotechnology experts to present to student audiences. The workshop includes a hands-on introduction to basic concepts in nanotechnology, and stimulates young audiences with a series of demonstrations and videos that highly exciting innovations and applications for nanotechnology! Ideal for audiences of up to 40 people.

Omni Nano Bridges

The Bridges program is perfect for large groups and provides an extended program for inspiring students with an education and entertaining series of demonstrations and tutorials highlighting current state-of-the-art applications of nanotechnology. Ideal for audiences of up to 200 people.

Omni Nano Curriculum

The flagship Omni Nano program is a comprehensive curriculum tailored for the needs of science educators. The Omni Nano curriculum provides an award-winning set of course materials, including syllabus, lecture outlines, lecture notes, slideshows, videos and quizzes. Our curriculum supports educators and provides a flexible framework, allowing the educator to focus on tailoring the curriculum to meet the specific needs of students, and find engaging ways to inspire students towards a career in nanotechnology!
