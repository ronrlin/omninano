About us Scientists and Educators

Who we are

Omni Nano develops high-quality, innovative, and interactive educational materials for teaching nanotechnology curricula to high school students. By providing a universally available and top-quality nanotechnology curriculum, we can do our best to inspire the next generation of scientists, entrepreneurs and engineers of tomorrow.

Why we do this

Student-centric

We haven't always been teachers. We were once students, inspired by teachers who introduced us to the power of knowledge at an early age.

Love of Science

It's said that knowledge is power. We want to empower every science educator with the tools to introduce the most cutting edge advances in nanotechnology right into their classrooms!

Proven Results

Our nanotechnology curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, while gaining exposure for one of the great scientific challenges of the coming decades. Students have responded overwhelmingly.
